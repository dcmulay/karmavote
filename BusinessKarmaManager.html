Donation History

Review, Pause and Restart your donations

TABLE 2 GOES HERE.


Columns are: 

  Donation received date

  Donation amount

  Payment reference

  Notes (as entered by business user while making payment)

  Payment method (iBanking, cheque, card gateway)

  Effective start date (manually set by KarmaVote Admin.   In future: automate start date triggered by bank credit email alert)

  Effective end date (counted in months from effective start date)

   Status - Active; Completed (i.e. end date < today); Paused (Business hidden from all pages, but prorata donation amount held with KarmaVote);   Awaiting Donation (payment not yet received); Stopped (stopped by KVAdmin - e.g. because of some reputation issue detected).

  Pause button - Visible only if donation status is Active and at least two days of donation remaining.  Display confirmation popup box.  Pausing will take effect on the next day.  The prorata donation amount remaining will be calculated and held on account until the Business agains presses Resume button.  

Resume button - Visible only if donation status is Paused.  Pressing Resume button will take effect on the same day as long as ther is some remaining donation balance.
